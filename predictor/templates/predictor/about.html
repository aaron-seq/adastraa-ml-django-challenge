Data Preprocessing

The application handles real-world data challenges:

  • Date Parsing: Multiple date formats (YYYY/MM/DD, DD-MM-YY, etc.)
  • Fuzzy Matching: Corrects typos in campaign names, locations, and keywords
  • Missing Values: Intelligent imputation strategies
  • Duplicates: Automatic detection and removal
  • Outliers: IQR-based outlier treatment
